About Quantum Encryption

This encryption system uses a unique dual-hole quantum encryption algorithm leveraging the black hole (138.0.0.1) and white hole (139.0.0.1) addresses for key generation.

Algorithm: QUANTUM-DUAL-HOLE-XOR-SHA3
Layer 1: XOR with Black Hole key
Layer 2: XOR with White Hole key (reverse)
Layer 3: SHA3-512 based permutation
Verification: SHA3-512 signature validation

All encrypted data includes a cryptographic signature to ensure message integrity and authenticity. The system is designed to be quantum-resistant.
